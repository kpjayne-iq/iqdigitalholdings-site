The Pipeline

A production engine, not a one-off.

IQ Video Studio runs on a proprietary pipeline that turns a brief into a finished video through four repeatable stages — engineered for consistency and speed. Studio-grade output, delivered faster and at a fraction of the cost of a traditional crew.

1

Brief & Script

We align on goal, audience, and format, then write a structured script built around a strong hook and a clear payoff.

2

Voice & Visuals

Narration is recorded and visuals are generated to a locked style — every frame on-brand and on-message.

3

Assembly & Motion

Everything is composited, timed, and rendered with motion design, titles, and brand framing applied automatically.

4

Delivery

You receive finished, publish-ready files — formatted for your platform, ready to upload the moment they land.
